Overview

Protect your property and occupants with a reliable fire sprinkler system. GPFS designs and installs systems for commercial and residential buildings, ensuring full compliance with NFPA and local codes. We also provide annual inspections, testing, and maintenance to keep your system ready.

What's included

  • Custom design for your building layout
  • Code-compliant installation
  • Annual inspection & maintenance plans
  • Reduced insurance premiums

How it works

1

Site Survey & Design

2

Common questions

Do I need a fire sprinkler system for my business?

Requirements vary by building type and occupancy. We can help you determine if a system is required or recommended.

How often should sprinkler systems be inspected?

NFPA 25 requires annual inspections. We offer maintenance plans to ensure your system stays compliant.
