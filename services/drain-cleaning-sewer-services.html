Drain Cleaning & Sewer Services

Professional drain cleaning and sewer line repair using advanced technology.

Schedule this service

Overview

Clogged drains and sewer backups are messy and stressful. GPFS uses hydro-jetting and video camera inspection to clear blockages and identify underlying issues. We repair or replace damaged sewer lines with trenchless technology when possible, saving your landscaping and driveway.

What's included

  • Video camera inspection for accurate diagnosis
  • Hydro-jetting removes grease, roots, and debris
  • Trenchless repair options to minimize digging
  • Preventative maintenance plans available

How it works

1

Camera Inspection

We insert a camera into the drain to locate the blockage and assess pipe condition.

2

Clearing the Blockage

Using hydro-jetting or auger, we remove the obstruction thoroughly.

3

Post-Clearing Inspection

We re-inspect with camera to confirm the line is clear and recommend repairs if needed.

Common questions

What causes sewer line backups?

Common causes include tree root intrusion, grease buildup, and pipe corrosion. Our camera inspection identifies the exact cause.

How long does drain cleaning take?

Most drain cleaning jobs are completed within 1-2 hours. Sewer line repairs may take longer depending on the method.
