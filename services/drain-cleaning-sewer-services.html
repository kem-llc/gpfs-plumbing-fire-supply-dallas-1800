Overview

Clogged drains and sewer backups are messy and stressful. GPFS uses hydro-jetting and video inspection to clear blockages and identify underlying issues. We repair or replace damaged sewer lines with minimal excavation.

What's included

  • Hydro-jetting for tough clogs
  • Video camera inspection for accuracy
  • Trenchless sewer repair options
  • Preventive maintenance plans

How it works

1

Inspection

We use a camera to locate the blockage and assess pipe condition.

2

Cleaning

Hydro-jetting or snaking clears the line thoroughly.

3

Repair or Maintenance

If needed, we repair damaged pipes or schedule regular maintenance.

Common questions

What causes recurring clogs?

Grease buildup, tree roots, or pipe damage often cause repeated clogs. We identify and fix the root cause.

Is hydro-jetting safe for old pipes?

Yes, our hydro-jetting is safe for most pipe materials. We adjust pressure based on pipe condition.
