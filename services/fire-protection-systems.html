Overview

GPFS is your full-service fire protection partner. We design and install fire sprinkler systems for commercial and residential properties, perform annual inspections, and repair fire pumps and backflow devices. Our licensed technicians ensure your system meets all local and national fire codes.

  • Extended system lifespan with maintenance

  • 24/7 emergency fire system repair
