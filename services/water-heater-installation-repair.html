Don't settle for cold showers. GPFS installs, repairs, and maintains all types of water heaters: tank, tankless, and hybrid. Common questions

How long does a water heater installation take?

A typical installation takes 2-4 hours. Tankless units may take longer due to venting and gas line modifications.

What size water heater do I need?

For a family of 4, a 50-gallon tank is standard. We'll calculate your peak hour demand to recommend the perfect size.
