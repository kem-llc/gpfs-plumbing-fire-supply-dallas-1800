Our story

Dallas's Trusted Plumbing & Fire Protection Team

To provide Dallas with reliable, honest, and expert plumbing and fire protection services that ensure safety, comfort, and peace of mind.

GPFS Plumbing & Fire Supply was founded in 1995 by a team of master plumbers and fire protection specialists who saw a need for a company that could handle both trades with equal expertise. Starting with a single truck, we built a reputation for quality work and honest communication. Over the years, we've grown into one of Dallas's most trusted service providers, serving thousands of homeowners and businesses.

Today, GPFS remains family-owned and operated. We invest in ongoing training, state-of-the-art equipment, and a customer-first approach. Whether it's a leaky faucet or a complex fire suppression system, we treat every job with the same commitment to excellence. Our team lives and works in Dallas, so we care about the community we serve.
