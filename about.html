About GPFS Plumbing & Fire Supply

Our mission is to provide Dallas with reliable plumbing and fire protection services that prioritize safety, quality, and customer satisfaction.

GPFS Plumbing & Fire Supply was founded with a simple vision: deliver exceptional service that Dallas residents and businesses can count on. With over 15 years of experience, our team has grown from a small operation to a trusted name in plumbing and fire protection. We've built our reputation on honesty, craftsmanship, and a commitment to doing the job right the first time.

Today, GPFS continues to serve the Dallas community with a full range of services, from emergency repairs to comprehensive fire suppression systems. Our licensed technicians stay up-to-date with the latest technology and codes, ensuring your property is safe and efficient. We're proud to be a local business that treats every customer like a neighbor.
