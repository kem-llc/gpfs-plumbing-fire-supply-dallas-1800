Our story

Your Dallas Partner in Plumbing & Fire Protection

To provide Dallas homeowners and businesses with reliable, code-compliant plumbing and fire protection services delivered with integrity, transparency, and exceptional craftsmanship.

GPFS Plumbing & Fire Supply was founded by a team of licensed plumbers and fire protection specialists who saw a gap in the market: companies that excelled in one area but couldn't offer integrated solutions. We combined our expertise to serve Dallas properties comprehensively, from fixing a leaky pipe to installing a full fire suppression system.

Today, we're proud to be a trusted name in the community. Our team continues to train on the latest technologies and codes, ensuring every job meets the highest standards. We believe in treating customers like neighbors — with respect, honesty, and a commitment to doing the job right the first time.
